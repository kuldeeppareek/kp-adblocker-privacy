Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal data. Period. This extension works entirely locally on your device.

1. Introduction

KP ad blocker ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles data and what information, if any, is collected.

2. Data Collection

We do NOT collect any data. Specifically:

  • ❌ No browsing history
  • ❌ No personal information
  • ❌ No cookies or tracking data
  • ❌ No IP addresses
  • ❌ No search queries
  • ❌ No website content
  • ❌ No usage statistics or analytics

3. How the Extension Works

KP ad blocker operates entirely locally on your device:

  • Content Filtering: The extension scans web pages in your browser and removes advertising elements based on predefined rules.
  • Request Blocking: Network requests to known advertising domains are blocked before they load.
  • Local Storage: Only the count of blocked ads is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This number never leaves your device.

4. Permissions Explanation

The extension requires certain permissions to function. Here's why:

  • webRequest & webNavigation: To intercept and block requests to advertising networks
  • storage: To save your blocked ads counter locally on your device
  • tabs: To inject ad-blocking scripts into web pages
  • declarativeNetRequest: To efficiently block ad network domains
  • <all_urls>: To block ads on all websites you visit
Important: None of these permissions are used to collect, transmit, or store your personal data.

5. Data Storage

The only data stored by this extension is:

  • Blocked Ads Count: A simple number stored locally that shows how many ads have been blocked during your browsing session.

This data:

  • ✓ Stays on your device only
  • ✓ Is never transmitted to external servers
  • ✓ Can be reset at any time via the extension popup
  • ✓ Is deleted when you uninstall the extension
